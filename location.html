How to Get There

The venue is easily accessible by public transport, car, and bike.

  • By Tube: Take the Central Line to "Liverpool Street Station." The venue is a 5-minute walk from the station.
  • By Car: Parking is limited, so we recommend using public transport. The nearest car park is NCP High Street.
  • By Bike: Bike racks are available at the venue entrance.
